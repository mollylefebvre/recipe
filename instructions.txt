Breakfast

Ingredients
- slice of bread
- 1 egg
- slice of cheddar
- spring onion
- bacon bites
- couple of cherry tomatoes

Step
1) Toast the slice of bread
2) Meanwhile cook the egg sunny side up
3) Put the slice of cheddar on top of the bread
4) Put the egg on top of the cheddar slice
5) Top the egg with spring onion and bacon bite
6) Serve with some cherry tomatoes on the side
